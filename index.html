Are you having a CRISIS?
If you or anyone is in immediate danger call 911

Helplines listed on this web application offer free, immediate and confidential one-on-one assistance to callers in need and usually operate 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. Crisis lines are provided as a service using information available on helpline provider's website. They will listen, offer support and advice, and if necessary, will try to help the caller get connected to help in their community.

If you find your self in a challenging emotional situation and need to speak to a trained counsellor, calling a helpline, crisis line, and or a suicide hotline offers a great way to be heard and get sound advice. You are not alone!

About

Fri, May 6 / Darren Heidt

Turning a Crisis into Peace of Mind

Early in 2021 I suffered from a series of concurrent panic attacks.During this series of panic attacks I struggled using my phone. I couldn’t figure out who to call or how. I tried to go through my email to find a recent email regarding mental health, I even tried google. I struggled to calm the panic. I struggled to figure out who to talk to. I struggled with what was real and what was noise. I struggled with everything.

It shouldn't have been so hard in those moments of crisis, it shouldn't be that difficult to find someone to talk to. Somehow this struggle could have been less traumatic.

From those struggles came this Web App that is easily accessible with just a QR code. I stapled it to my wall and carried it with me early on in the development. It Gave me some peace of mind whenever the chaotic noise came back. Knowing I wouldn’t struggle if I needed help.

Take the qr code, apply it, use it, print it on business cards, appointment cards, use as part of health and safety or first aid training, distribute it in any way you believe will help a person in crisis. Help someone find someone to talk to when they may need it the most.

If this helps one person find hope when it seems lost. Then all the effort, long days and madness will be worth it.

Download QR Code for free! No login or email required, just a wish to help someone in need.
