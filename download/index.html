Download QR code

Download free Qr code and add to any printed materials. QR code can be used on business cards, appointment slips, public areas, newspapers...etc anywhere. Always include appropriate crisis helpline number with QR code. Use QR code anywhere somebody may need to contact a crisis helpline. Print and paste it to your wall if you are not well and you may need someones ear. Save to your phone, if you find someone in distress. These actions may assist someone in a crisis and make a difference in their day!
